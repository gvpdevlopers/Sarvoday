FREQUENTLY ASKED

Questions & Answers

What is S9 Sarvoday Doctor Park?

S9 Sarvoday Doctor Park is a premium medical-commercial development designed specifically for doctors, healthcare professionals, clinics, consultants, diagnostic centers, pharmacies, and healthcare businesses.

Where is the project located?

The project is strategically located in Palanpur, North Gujarat, offering excellent connectivity and accessibility for doctors, patients, and healthcare businesses.

Are commercial shops and doctor units available?

Yes, the project offers premium doctor units and modern commercial showrooms designed for healthcare-focused businesses. These spaces are ideal for clinics, consultants, pharmacies, diagnostic centers, wellness brands, and other medical-related services.

Is parking available within the project?

Yes, S9 Sarvoday Doctor Park includes dedicated parking facilities for both owners and visitors.

What is the current construction status?

The project is currently nearing completion, with major infrastructure and development work already in advanced stages.
